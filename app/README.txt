HOW TO OPEN/RUN THE SITE
------------------------
1. Open the folder containing these files
2. Double-click on "index.html" to open in your web browser
   OR
   Right-click "index.html" and select "Open with" -> Your Browser

That's it! No server, no build step, no installation needed.
The site works entirely in the browser.
